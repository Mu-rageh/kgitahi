About Us

Our Team

Welcome to Kimani Gitahi & Associates, a distiguished firm of Certified Public Accountants with a proven track record in auditing and financial management.

Established in 1992, our firm boasts a rich legacy of expertise, professionalism and excellence with a team of highly skilled audit partners, experienced senior staff and dedicated professionals. We offer tailored audit services that adhere to the highest industry standards.

We recognize that each client has unique needs. Our audit approach and methodology are agile, ensuring that we align our services precisely with your objectives and scope. Key elements of our approach include:


  • Risk-Based Analysis: Our experts identify and assess risks, tailoring the audit approach to effectively mitigate these concerns.

  • Strategic Planning: Senior members of our team engage in meticulous audit planning, ensuring a well-structured and efficient process.

  • Open Communication: Throughout the audit, we maintain constant communication, ensuring you're informed every step of the way.

  • Value-Adding Reporting: Our reports provide accurate, consistent and value-added insights that empower your management.

Objectives and Commitment


Our paramount goal is to deliver audit services that exceed expectations. We pledge to provide thorough and insightful audit certifications of project expenditure, inventory and cash. Additionaly, our management letter will encompass valuable observations and recommendations to enhance project activities.

We approach each assignment with the mindest of our building enduring partnerships. Through our unwavering dedication, expertise and professionalism, we seek to establish lasting relationships with our horizons, we look forward to the opportunity to serve your auditing needs and contribute to your organization's success.
